# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment: using welcoming
language, respecting differing viewpoints, accepting constructive criticism
gracefully, and focusing on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior
includes harassment, trolling, insulting comments, and publishing others' private
information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
privately to the repository owner through
[GitHub](https://github.com/AgriciDaniel). All complaints are reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.
